Title: This Tiny Submarine Cruises Inside A Stomach To Deliver Drugs
Author: Madeline K. Sofia

Scientists have created an experimental device that putters around inside the 
stomach, neutralizing acid and then delivering antibiotics. The goal is to help
the antibiotics work better.
