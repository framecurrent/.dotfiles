Title: Catch-bond mechanism of the bacterial adhesin FimH
Author: 
Date: Mon, 07 Mar 2016 00:00:00 +0100
Link: http://feeds.nature.com/~r/ncomms/rss/current/~3/YoaObx9ilEs/ncomms10738

Article

Catch bonds have a role in bacterial adhesion and infection by uropathogenic E.
coli. Here, the authors report crystal structures, molecular dynamics 
simulations, ligand binding analysis and cell tracking to characterise the 
catch bond interaction between the adhesin FimH and carbohydrate receptors.

Nature Communications doi: 10.1038/ncomms10738[1]

Authors: Maximilian M. Sauer, Roman P. Jakob, Jonathan Eras, Sefer Baday, Deniz
Eriş, Giulio Navarra, Simon Bernèche, Beat Ernst, Timm Maier, Rudi Glockshuber
