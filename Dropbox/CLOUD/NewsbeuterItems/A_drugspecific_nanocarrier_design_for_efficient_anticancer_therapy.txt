Title: A drug-specific nanocarrier design for efficient anticancer therapy
Author: 
Date: Thu, 09 Jul 2015 03:00:00 +0200
Link: http://feeds.nature.com/~r/ncomms/rss/current/~3/GIYynQp_iW0/ncomms8449

Article

Telodendrimers are versatile and robust nanoparticle-based drug carriers. From 
a screen of potential small-molecule building blocks, Shi et al. identify 
rhein-containing telodendrimers as stable and effective nanocarriers of 
doxorubicin for treating a xenograft Raji lymphoma model.

Nature Communications doi: 10.1038/ncomms8449[1]

Authors: Changying Shi, Dandan Guo, Kai Xiao, Xu Wang, Lili Wang, Juntao Luo
