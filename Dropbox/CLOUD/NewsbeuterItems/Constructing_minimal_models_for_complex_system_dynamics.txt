Title: Constructing minimal models for complex system dynamics
Author: 
Date: Wed, 20 May 2015 03:00:00 +0200
Link: http://feeds.nature.com/~r/ncomms/rss/current/~3/pakEdO9khAM/ncomms8186

Article

In statistical physics, the observable macroscopic behaviour of a system is 
obtained from a microscopic model of its components. Here, the authors extend 
this approach to systems with no known microscopic dynamics, by looking at the 
system’s response to external perturbations.

Nature Communications doi: 10.1038/ncomms8186[1]

Authors: Baruch Barzel, Yang-Yu Liu, Albert-László Barabási
